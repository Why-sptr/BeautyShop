Deskripsi

MSH Niacinamide Brightening Moisture Gel, with its lightweight texture, absorbs quickly and helps in oil control. Formulated with the novel SKINTIFIC exclusive MSH Niacinamide combined with two lightweight and highly effective brightening agents, Alpha Arbutin and Tranexamic Acid. Helps in significantly brightening the skin. Clinically proven to be 10 times more effective than regular niacinamide in reducing dark spots and blackheads.
